About the Consultancy

We specialise in conducting high-level, independent reviews into unexpected deaths and suicides within clinical and community settings. Our approach is defined by a commitment to the Duty of Candour and the pursuit of system-wide learning.

Our lead consultants bring decades of experience from backgrounds in clinical governance, legal investigation, and mental health advocacy. We understand the complexities of multi-agency working and the sensitivities required when engaging with bereaved families.
